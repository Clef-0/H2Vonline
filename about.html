What is it?

ElDewrito is a community-made mod for Halo Online, a cancelled free Halo 3 derived PC game for the Russian market. It allows the game to be played in player-hosted servers and aims to restore removed features, fix bugs and glitches, and add mod support.

In The Press

"The hardcore few who prefer to play Halo on their own terms." - Andy Maxwell, TorrentFreak

Built by the Halo Community

ElDewrito was created through the hard work of well known Halo fans, developers and modders like DEElekgolo, Gamecheat13, Lord Zedd and many others.

Fan Favourite Maps

Featuring classic Halo 3 maps like Valhalla and Guardian alongside brand new maps Edge and Reactor with an assortment of community creations on top.

Truly Free

ElDewrito can be played for free and is open source, allowing you to contribute to a real community project.
